ING Real Estate Finance, Netherlands – SAP Banking Implementation

IBM (Netherlands & India) have signed this US$ 5.3 Million contract for end-to-end implemenatation of SAP Core Banking Modules. This is one of the first such end-to-end SAP Core Banking Implementation which IBM as a whole have won.

The project during is from July-2008 to Sept-2009 and likely to go upto Q1-2010 and being rolled out in 7 countries
(Spain/Italy/Netherlands /France/Germany/UK/USA)

The total project team size is 28 as of today, with 12 SAP Banking Consultants and ABAP Consultants from IBM India GD. The remaining are from IBM NL and 4 Consultants from SAP.

IBM is involved in Review of Blueprints, Realisation, Go-Live, Rollout and Sustain Phases.

The following modules of SAP Corebanking are being implemented

• SAP FI/CO
• Business Partner
• Loans Management
• Collateral Management
• Solution Manager
• Adobe Interactive Forms
• Enhancements and Interfaces Development in ABAP

IBM India GD is operating from 3 locations as of today, Bangalore/Pune/Hyderabad and a few onsite presence at Den Haag. Network connectivity between these 3 offshore locations and ING Den Haag has been established and operational.
