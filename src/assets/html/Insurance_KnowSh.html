Prudential’s participation in the recent sharenets

The participation from Prudential account has always been overwhelming in all the organization wide events, be it sharenets, outings, Imprint events etc…

This time Prudential proved it’s mettle by participating in the number of sharenets organized in the recent past.

Testing sharenet held on 12th Sep 2008  A total of 56 abstracts were submitted in Bangalore out of which 12 were contributed by the Prudential team. 2 out of the 12 made it for the final presentation and they are –

  • Testing Web Services in SOA by Ramya Nandakumar
  • Testing Methodology – A Modern Approach Padmavathy Gopal

Insurance Conclave held on 25th Sep 2008 3 abstracts were submitted by Prudential team members, out of which 2 made it for the final event –

  • Demo on Straight Through processing by Subrahmanya G Somyaji
  • Animation on Retirement Insurance by Pradeep Atmakuri

Mainframe Sharenet held on 29th August 2008 A total of 3 abstarcts were submitted by Prudential for the Mainframe Sharenet held in Chennai -

  • Report Compare Utility for Mainframes by Kishore Reddy
  • System Efficiency through REXX  ISPF Macros by Radhika Bandi
  • Magic of REXX Macros by Trilok Kumar
