DTCC in Insurance Sector


Overview:

Depository Trust and Clearing Corporation (DTCC) was established in 1999 as a holding company to combine Depository Trust Corporation (DTC) and National Securities Clearing Corporation (NSCC). Its primary business is to provide an efficient and safe way for buyers and sellers of securities to make their exchange, and thus "clear and settle" transactions. It also provides custody of securities. DTCC provides clearance, settlement, and information services for the financial sector.  It is also playing a leading role as processor of mutual funds, insurance transactions, linking funds and carriers with their distribution network.
