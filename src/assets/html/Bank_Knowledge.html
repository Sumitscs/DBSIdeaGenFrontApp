Domain Knowledge: Global Banking Standardst

Purpose of Global Banking Standards:

The main purpose or theme of this document is to broadly layout the information regarding the "Global Banking Standards" which drives home the complex nature of the global banking regulatory measures like:

• BASEL Compliance,
• Advanced Internal Rating Based (AIRB) systems and
• Anti-Monetary Laundering (AML) policies

It is indeed delightful to know that these concepts and regulatory measures are being used by most of the Tier 1, Tier 2 as well as Tier 3 banks and financial institutions in US, Europe as well as Asia.
