One more feather in Prudential’s cap


Prudential team over the years has won client confidence which resulted in a new project   “Service Workflow Automation” coming our way in May 2008. The highlight of the project is that it is based on Service Oriented Architecture (SOA).

The project brought in a lot of opportunity for the Prudential account to grow both in terms of resources and technology.

  • It helped in adding 20 more members in the team.
  • It is developed using Pega system’s BPM(Business Process Management) tool called Pega Rules Process Commander (PRPC). Business Process Management refers to a set of activities which organizations can perform to either optimize their business processes or adapt them to new organizational needs.

But, as we know all new things bring along some initial obstacles and challenges. Following were some of the challenges in front of the SWA team –

  • Team Ramp up for PEGA Developers & Skill set within a short period of time.
  • Acquiring Domain/ Functional Knowledge.
  • Managing Customer expectations.
  • Team co-ordination from multiple locations involving Onsite and Offshore.

 

The team emerged successfully out of the initial challenges and has already delivered Release 1 processes and has won a lot of appreciations from the customer for all their hard work.
