Hong Kong Core Banking CASA Migration

In Apr 2017, DBS HK successfully migrated its Current Account, Saving Account, and Deposit application module from its Systematics core banking system on mainframe platform to Finacle core banking system on AIX platform. This was a crucial moment of the journey of Hong Kong Core Banking Project with the joint effort by our strong and dedicated team here in DBS.

In this project, HK AMS team worked closely with DBS project team in all project phases (e.g. solutioning consultancy, data migration, system interface enhancements, re-platform of e-Channels & new user functionalities, integrated system testing, integrated UAT, dress rehearsals, pilot runs, and etc) on and all aspects of system touch points (e.g. system & data migration, system integration, batch job enhancements, system decommissioning, etc) of all IBM supported applications on all platforms (e.g. mainframe, open systems, e-channels, branch teller systems, ATM, data warehouse, etc)

HK AMS SMEs support the respective tracks, providing the professional advice to help DBS teams to define the system features and customization requirements, development, testing and implementation. HK AMS team demonstrated the quick response and flexibility to the changes of requirements, and changes of overall project plan on testing, dressed rehearsal, and implementation from the client. During the testing and implementation phases, AMS teams maintained a quality work, keeping the number of defects in low level, so the client project team could focus the attention to handle the other important matters in the project delivery. The team also maintained a very stable production environment for all its AMS-supported mainframe applications, before, during and after the cutover. This enabled both AMS delivery teams and client application teams to free up its resources for the migration project.



 

Iconic Journey

Iconic Journey is a very important DBS HK business initiative to enhance customer experience when applying DBS HK credit cards and to transform business & operational process for handling customer credit card applications more efficiently through digitization process.

IBM HK was chosen as the business partner by DBS to work closely with DBS project team, comprising of both DBS business project team and IT project team in all phases, including brain-storming of business & use cases, customer survey, business requirements, technical solutioning, and project implementation.

Iconic journey has now launched a few major releases to enhance customer experiences, from initial PC browser version of credit card application forms, then support of mobile/pad responsive forms, and now credit card application forms and workflow fully integrated with DBS Omni mobile application. Throughout this project, Agile approach was adopted to meet the very dynamic DBS HK business needs and Go-to-Market requirements for early delivery of values to customers (e.g. enhanced credit card application flow, add-on card flow, card application save & resume flow, document upload flow, DBS Direct Sales version, and etc).

With the successful rollout of various Icon Journey releases, new DBS credit card application requests of customers through various digitized channels has now increased from single digit to over 60% from its traditional channels. This is a very successful business driven program out of DBS and DBS business management is now considering to apply the same Iconic Journey to other key growing business areas.



 


Ellie - Watson Virtual Assistant

In Jun 2017, DBS HK engaged IBM HK business partner to perform a 2-week POC on Chatbot in line with the financial market trend of using more and more Chatbot to enhance customer experience. The DBS chatbot "Ellie" was borne in this POC to provide Natural Language capability (empowered by IBM Watson Conversation Service), and was trained with knowledge of 80 general FAQs related to DBS credit card business to handle online customer enquiries instantly on 24x7 basis.

Ellie today can help customers calculate DBS$ in DBS credit card reward program, direct customers to go to nearest DBS branch on the spot, share latest DBS credit card marketing campaign info in 1-click.

After this initial POC, DBS HK further engaged IBM HK with another POC. This POC is to enhance Ellie with Speech Analytics capability to analyze customer call logs to allow Ellie to upgrade its knowledge continuously. 150 customer call logs from DBS call center was selected and IBM HK used Watson Content Analytics to analyze it and trained Ellie. After 1st round of training, Ellie's accuracy level of response improved from 61% to 80%.



DBS HK is now discussing with IBM HK how to bring Ellie to production stage and official business launch.

 


Kill Paper - DataCap

Big offices in DBS HK Central office is currently occupied for storing paper. DBS employees are holding large piles of paper files in their hands and performing time-consuming day-to-day jobs manually with heavy reliance on these hardcopy paper documents. This is how the "Kill Paper" project was started in DBS HK.

Our team approached DBS team, understood their pain point and requirement, and quickly came up with a win-win solution - introducing IBM Datacap to help our client! With the strong OCR capability with Datacap, we successfully helped 3 business groups in DBS to design the business flow as well as building template for them to enable kill paper project at their end too.

Kill paper project is more than a project - it is a digital transformation that we are bringing true values to client, and how we use advanced technology to create a paperless ecosystem within DBS. With Datacap, user can quickly search for the document they need with a few clicks - no more headache to find a document they need anywhere and anytime.



Today, we are talking about how we kill paper. Tomorrow, we will be talking about streamlining process workflow through automation and providing management insights for decision making out of powerful Watson cognitive analytics capability. Say goodbye to paper, and let's welcome the paperless era here.

 


Automation

Since 2016, AMS HK team has continuously invested in various process automation and transformation to improve in cost efficiency, productivity, and enhancing customer experience through faster problem alert, faster trouble shooting and system recovery. Just to name a few examples below:

Automation - 24x7 Application Monitoring : AMS HK team automated transaction performance detection for DBS HK Mobile Banking, Internet Banking and Internet stock trading applications which improved stability of DBS HK applications and reduced problem determination & system recovery turn-around time after DBS HK CASA migration implementation.

Automation - Application Health Checks AMS HK team enhanced their automated application health check process to support: (1) individual instance level check and (2) all instances check. This empowered AMS HK support team with higher flexibility when supporting various type of system changes in production. AMS HK team implemented automation scripts to check for completeness and status of all DBS HK mobile banking batch jobs.

Automation - DC operations and manually monitored batch jobs (coming soon)

AMS HK team is working with various DBS HK Application Managers to implement TWS batch job automation & monitoring for those IBM supported applications which are still running in production with TWS monitoring. This is another important step moving forward towards true automation which can further enhance production stability and enable faster system recovery.
